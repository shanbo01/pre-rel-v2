Facts and Figures

  • Found in 1998
  • Located in Istanbul, Turkey
  • Around 17,048 students across all campuses

  • More than 100 degree programs in English and Turkish language

Requirements:

  • Passport
  • Passport ID photo
  • High school diploma and grades
  • Motivational letter (only for medicine and dentistry applicants)
  • English language certificate (if available)

Important notes

  • the university has the right to ask for extra documents if needed

  • if the student do not have an English language certificate, you will attend an English language proficiency test
