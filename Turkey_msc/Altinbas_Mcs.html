Facts and Figures

  • Found in 2008
  • Located in Istanbul, Turkey
  • Around 11,450 students across all campuses
  • Has 3 campuses across Turkey
  • Offers more than 70-degree programs

Requirements:

  • Passport
  • Passport ID photo (White or blue background)
  • Bachelor degree and transcripts
  • High school diploma and transcripts
  • Recommendation letter
  • Motivational letter (for dentistry and medicine students ONLY)
  • English language certificate (if available)

Important notes

  • the university has the right to ask for extra documents if needed

  • if the student do not have an English language certificate, you will attend an English language proficiency test
